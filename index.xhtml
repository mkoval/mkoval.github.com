Who am I?

I am a junior at Rutgers University who is double-majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering and Computer Science. My primary academic interests are in artificial intelligence and computer vision, with a strong focus on their application to robotics. As part of my interest in robotics, I am currently serving as the Vice President of Rutgers University's IEEE Student Branch and am responsible for the vision system of Rutgers Navigator, our entry into the 2011 Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition.

You can read about my contributions to the Rutgers Navigator, my research as part of the Rutgers Real Life Reinforcement Learning Laboratory, and miscellaneous personal projects in the "projects" section of this website.
